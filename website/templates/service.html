Services

Dental care services encompass a wide range of preventive, diagnostic, and treatment options to ensure the overall health of the oral cavity. Here are common services offered by dental care providers:

Routine Dental Examinations:

Regular check-ups for oral health assessments.

Dental Cleanings:

Professional removal of plaque and tartar to prevent cavities and gum disease.

X-rays and Diagnostic Imaging:

Radiographic examinations for a detailed view of teeth and jaw structures.

Preventive Services:

Application of fluoride treatments to strengthen teeth.

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings for cavities (using materials like composite, amalgam, or porcelain).

Orthodontic Services

Braces or clear aligners to correct misaligned teeth or bite issues.
